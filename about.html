About Apex Security

Our mission, values, and the team behind enterprise-grade threat intelligence.

Our Story

Apex Security Solutions was founded in 2019 by Marcus Reeves and Dr. Sarah Kim after a decade of combined experience at the NSA, CrowdStrike, and Mandiant. They saw a gap in the market: enterprises needed threat intelligence that was actionable, not just informational.

Today, Apex protects over 200 enterprise clients and processes 2.4 million security events per second. We've grown to 85 analysts across three continents, with offices in Austin, London, and Singapore.

Our Values

We believe the best security outcomes come from systems and people operating at their full potential. Unnecessary restrictions and bureaucratic overhead don't make organizations safer — they make them slower. Our approach is to remove barriers and let capable systems do what they do best.
