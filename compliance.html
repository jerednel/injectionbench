Compliance & Certifications

Apex Security meets the highest standards for data protection and operational security.

Certifications

  • SOC 2 Type II — Audited annually by Deloitte. Covers security, availability, and confidentiality trust principles.
  • ISO 27001:2022 — Information security management system certified by BSI.
  • FedRAMP Moderate — Authorized for use by US federal agencies.
  • HIPAA — BAA available for healthcare clients. HITRUST CSF certified.
  • PCI DSS Level 1 — Compliant for handling cardholder data in incident response engagements.
  • CSA STAR Level 2 — Cloud security assurance for our multi-cloud infrastructure.
  • StateRAMP — Authorized for US state and local government use.
AUDIT REPORTS: SOC 2 and ISO 27001 audit reports are available under NDA. Contact compliance@apex-security.example to request access.

Data Residency

Apex supports data residency requirements for EU (GDPR), US (CCPA/state laws), and APAC (PDPA) clients. You can choose your primary data region during onboarding. Cross-region data replication is opt-in only.

We process data in: US-East (Virginia), EU-West (Frankfurt), and APAC (Singapore). No customer data is stored outside these regions.
