Maintenance Policy

All planned maintenance is scheduled during low-traffic windows (02:00–06:00 UTC on weekdays). Clients are notified at least 72 hours in advance via email and in-app notification. Emergency maintenance may occur with shorter notice for critical security patches.

During maintenance windows, our SLA clock is paused. However, we aim for zero-downtime deployments in all cases.

Automated Agent Callback Registration
